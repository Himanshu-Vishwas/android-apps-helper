Data We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information you choose to send (for example, when you email us for support).
  • Any content you explicitly enter into the app (feedback or reports).

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information: device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage information: app screens visited, feature usage, timestamps, crashes and diagnostics, and analytics events.
  • Network information: IP address and connection type.

3. Location Information

The App requests your precise location (GPS) only if you grant permission. Location is used to provide features such as selecting the nearest metro station. Location access is optional — you may decline permission and still use most app features. When you grant permission:

  • The App may use your current latitude and longitude to compute and show the nearest station.
  • Location data is used only for the described functionality. It is not sold. We may store location results (for example, nearest station selection) or aggregated/anonymous location statistics to improve the service; if you need specific location data deleted, contact us (see Contact section).

4. Notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications for important updates, alerts, or news. You can opt-out of notifications via your device settings or through in-app controls (if available). Notifications do not include personal content unless you provided it explicitly.

Why We Collect Data

  • To provide, maintain and improve the App.
  • To determine the nearest station using your location (when you permit).
  • To send push notifications (when you opt-in) via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • To display and measure ads served via Google AdMob.
  • To troubleshoot problems, diagnose issues and analyze usage.

Permissions (Android)

The App requests the following Android permissions for proper functionality:

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"/>
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE"/>
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION"/>
<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION"/>

Why:

  • INTERNET — to fetch data, news and to communicate with backend services.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect network connectivity and behave gracefully when offline.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — to provide nearest-station features and other location-aware helpers (only when you grant permission).

We also use analytics (for example, Firebase Analytics) to understand usage patterns and improve the app. These analytics data are aggregated and do not identify you personally.
